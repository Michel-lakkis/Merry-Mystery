How MerryMystery Works

Get ready to bring some holiday magic to your friends and family with MerryMystery! Here’s how it works:

  1. Gather all the emails and names of the participants, including your own.
  2. Enter the total number of players in the input field.
  3. Provide the names and corresponding email addresses for everyone.
  4. Click the "Send Invitations" button, and we’ll take care of the rest!

Each participant will receive an email from the MerryMystery team, revealing the name of the person they’ll be gifting. It’s simple, fun, and a great way to spread joy this holiday season!
